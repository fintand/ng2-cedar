Welcome to Cedar Driving School

Cedar Driving School was established in 1972. Our fully qualified RSA and ADI instructors boast over years’ experience in driving instruction and testing in Ireland. Cedar driving school offers individually tailored driving courses to learners of all levels, while also specialising in Essential Driver Training tuition (EDT). We also specialise in pre-test courses provided by a former driving tester with over 20 years’ experience who can ensure you are receiving instruction of the highest standard which will enable you to meet the requirements of the driving test. Cedar Driving School strives to offer high quality driving lessons at reasonable prices with lessons starting from as little as 30 euro.
